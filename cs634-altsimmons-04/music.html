DISCOGRAPHY

El Condor Pasa 100 Anos

Exclusive album produced by Sylvia Falcón for the Peruvian label Play Music & Video, brings together artists such as Luis Alva, William Luna, Susana Baca, Qori Ñusta, Alborada, Justino Alvarado, Carlos Guerrero, the maestros Raymond Thevenot and Daniel Kirwayo, the Symphony Orchestra of Cusco and the Philharmonic Orchestra of Lima. It contains an extensive article by Sylvia Falcón about the history of the filmmakers of the famous zarzuela and the context of its premiere.

Killa Lluqsimun - Special Edition

Impressive record, exquisite voice of the Pachamama. One of the best Andean music records in a special edition. It includes traditional songs from the Peruvian Andes and contemporary versions of the classics of Yma Sumac, K'arawi and Mamallay, wonderful theme, live bonus track of this edition.

Killa Lluqsimun

Concept, direction and general production: Sylvia Falcón. It was recorded in the city of Lima between December 2006 and June 2007 at QB Studios, with the exception of issues 2 and 11, recorded in Decibel studios, on the first day of November 2005, by Robert Chávez. Mixed and mastered at QB Studios, August 2007.
